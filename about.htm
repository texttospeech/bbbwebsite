Our Goal

Our goal is to educate the world about correctly dealing with trash. We hope to make the world a better, greener place by helping people deal with their waste in the way that is best for the environment, in order to make a better world for future generations.

About us, the Banana Bot Builders

We are the Banana Bot Builders, a FLL team. For this year we are doing the challenge: Trash Trek. We talked to Waste Management officer Paul Burns. He said that the biggest problem was educating people to recycle, compost, etc. correctly. So we created this website.

FLL

FLL is an international 'lego league' a competition based on lego robotics and a project that varies from year to year. This year the challenge is 'Trash Trek.' This means we have to solve a problem about trash. As you can see above, we decided to solve the prooblem of educating people about what to do with what waste.
