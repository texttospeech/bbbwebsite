The Environmental Protection Departmet launched a new program called: "Clink, Drink then Recycle" as of October 2015. "Clink, Drink then Recycle" is a glass bottle recycling program that is being staged at major shopping malls and commercial buildings on Hong Kong Island. The idea is to encourage restaurants and food stands at these malls to join glass bottle recycling. These commercial buildings and malls will help the restaurants and food stands in sorting their glass bottles and storing them centrally. Enviromental Protection Department's Contractor will deliver the gathered glass bottles to local recyclers that turn the bottles into glass sand for the making of construction maerials. Now everyone can support glass recycling when eating out!
